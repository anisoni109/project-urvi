Climate Refugees

As climate change accelerates, many people are being displaced from their homes due to rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and other environmental factors.

Case Studies

1. The Maldives: With its low-lying islands, the Maldives is facing existential threats from rising sea levels.

2. Bangladesh: Annual flooding displaces millions of people, forcing them to seek refuge elsewhere.

Conclusion

We must take urgent action to mitigate climate change and support those displaced by it.
